Our Mission

Energy that moves with real life

Too many backup systems are outdated, hard to move, expensive to maintain, or not built for how people actually use power in India. We're building an energy platform that's easier to deploy, easier to scale, and easier to live with.

The problem we're solving

India faces over 10 billion hours of power outages annually. Existing solutions noisy diesel generators, static lead-acid inverters, or expensive imported power stations leave a massive gap. Homes, shops, schools, resorts, and field operations all need something better: portable, clean, smart, and affordable.

Our approach

We combine LiFePO4 battery technology, intelligent power management, solar readiness, and product design thinking into a unified platform. From a 6.5 kg personal unit to a modular infrastructure rack one product ecosystem that scales with real demand. Made in India, for India.
